Dataset: Gender Gap in Computational Biology Analysis (2015-2025)
Description: Complete datasets for reproducing the analysis
Contents:
- pubmed_biology_2015_2025.csv: Biology papers from PubMed
- pubmed_compbio_2015_2025.csv: Computational biology papers
- gender_data.db.gz: SQLite database with inferred gender data
- scimagojr_2024_archived.csv: Journal impact quartiles (snapshot)
- gender_cache.json: Cached gender classifications
